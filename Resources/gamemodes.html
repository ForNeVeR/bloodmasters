Bloodmasters

The basic goal is to frag (kill) your opponents to get frags (score). But the goal may differ a bit for each Game Type.

Deathmatch
Frag your opponents to score. The winner is the player who first hits the Fraglimit or the player with the highest score when the Timelimit is hit. Weapons, items and powerups can be found throughout the entire map. When a player gets fragged, the player respawns and gets 100% health, default weapon but no powerups.

Team Deathmatch
Frag your opponents to score for your team. The winner is the team who first hits the fraglimit or the team with the highest score when timelimit is hit. Weapons, items and powerups can be found throughout the entire map. When a player gets fragged, the player respawns and gets 100% health, default weapon but no powerups.

Last Man Standing
Frag your opponents and try to keep alive. The winner is the last player alive and will get 1 point (score). No weapons, items or powerups can be found in the map. When a player gets fragged, the player enters spectator mode. Players in spectator mode cannot join the game and will join the game automatically next round. When a round starts, all joining players get 100% health, All weapons but no powerups.

Last Team Standing
Frag your opponents and try to keep alive. The winner is the last team alive and will get 1 point (score). No weapons, items or powerups can be found in the map. When a player gets fragged, the player enters spectator mode. Players in spectator mode cannot join the game and will join the game automatically next round. When a round starts, all joining players get 100% health, All weapons but no powerups.

Red Rover
Frag your opponents to score and try to get all players in your team. The winner is the player with the highest score when only 1 team is left. No weapons, items or powerups can be found in the map. When a player gets fragged, it will join the team of the player who fragged, respawns and gets 100% health, All weapons but no powerups.

Capture the Flag
Frag your opponents to score, try to get the flag of the opponent team, drop the flag on the spot in our base when your flags is there to capture. The winner is the team who first hits the capturelimit, or the the team with the most captures when timelimit is hit. Weapons, items and powerups can be found throughout the map. When a player gets fragged, the player respawns within its own base and gets 100% health, default weapon but no powerups.

Scavenger
Capture (collect) items throughout the map to score. You can frag your opponents to keep them from collecting the items. The winner is the player who first hits the capturelimit, or the player with the most captures when timelimit is hit. Weapons, items and powerups can be found throughout the map. When a player gets fragged, the player respawns and gets 100% health, default weapon but no powerups.

Team Scavenger
Capture (collect) items throughout the map to score for your Team. The items are colored and you must only capture the items of your team. Capturing an item of the opponent team will cost you 1 point. You can frag your opponents to keep them from collecting the items. The winner is the team who first hits the capturelimit, or the team with the most captures when timelimit is hit. Weapons, items and powerups can be found throughout the map. When a player gets fragged, the player respawns and gets 100% health, default weapon but no powerups.
